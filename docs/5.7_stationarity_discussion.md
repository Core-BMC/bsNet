## 논문 반영 전략 (확정)

### 1. Methods 섹션

> "The Spearman-Brown (SB) prophecy formula assumes that split halves
> represent **parallel tests** — i.e., equal true scores and error
> variances across halves. We evaluated this assumption using sliding
> window analysis on synthetic data (7 non-overlapping windows of
> ~17 samples each within the 120-sample short scan)."

수치를 직접 보고:
> "Inter-window FC correlation was 0.66 ± 0.01, indicating moderate
> temporal consistency. The ICC(2,1) was −0.16, suggesting that the
> strict parallel test assumption is not fully met."

### 2. Discussion/Limitations 섹션 (핵심)

방어 논증 3단계:

**(a) 한계를 투명하게 인정:**
> "The negative ICC indicates that the strict parallel test assumption
> underlying the SB formula is violated in our short-scan fMRI context.
> This is expected, as fMRI time series exhibit non-stationarity due to
> physiological fluctuations and scanner drift."

**(b) Cheng et al. (2021) 선례로 정당화:**
> "However, Cheng et al. (2021) demonstrated that CTT-based reliability
> correction (using the same SB framework) improved FC estimates by
> +0.33 ICC points in HCP data (N = 1,003), despite similarly
> approximate parallel test conditions. They concluded that the SB
> formula provides 'pragmatically useful' corrections even when the
> parallel test assumption is only approximately met."
>
> DOI: 10.1016/j.neuroimage.2021.118005

**(c) 경험적 증거로 뒷받침:**
> "Our Component Necessity Analysis (Table X) confirms this pragmatic
> utility: removing the SB component caused the largest performance
> degradation (Δρ̂T = −0.307), demonstrating that the SB correction
> provides substantial empirical benefit despite the theoretical
> limitation."
