### Figure 8: Single-Seed BS-NET Validation on ADHD-200 — CC200 (N=40, Fisher z)
**File**: `Figure8_ADHD_SingleSeed_CC200.png`
**Script**: `src/scripts/plot_adhd_results.py --csv data/adhd/results/adhd_bsnet_cc200.csv`

**(a) Raw FC vs BS-NET Prediction**: Scatter plot colored by diagnostic group (blue = Control, red = ADHD). Both groups show consistent upward displacement from the identity line, indicating group-agnostic correction.
**(b) Improvement by Group**: Box-and-strip plot of Δ = ρ̂T − r_FC, stratified by ADHD/Control. No significant between-group difference: ADHD Δ = +0.046, Control Δ = +0.048, confirming unbiased correction.
**(c) Distribution Comparison**: Overlaid histograms of r_FC (gray) and ρ̂T (blue), demonstrating the rightward shift from raw to corrected values. Mean r_FC = 0.819 → ρ̂T = 0.866.
**(d) BS-NET with 95% CI (sorted)**: Per-subject ρ̂T with bootstrap confidence intervals, sorted ascending. Group coloring confirms interleaved distribution — no systematic group-level bias.

**Key statistics**: N=40, CC200 → 195 ROIs, r_FC = 0.819 ± 0.090, ρ̂T = 0.866 ± 0.047, 77.5% improved.

### Figure 10: Multi-Seed BS-NET on ADHD-200 — CC200 (N=40, 10 seeds, Fisher z)

### Figure 12: Multi-Atlas Comparison — ADHD-200 (CC200 vs CC400)
**File**: `Figure12_ADHD_Atlas_Comparison.png`
**Script**: `src/scripts/plot_adhd_results.py --atlas-compare`

**(a) ρ̂T Distribution by Atlas**: Overlaid histograms comparing CC200 (μ = 0.866) and CC400 (μ = 0.855) ρ̂T distributions. Near-complete overlap indicates atlas-agnostic correction.
**(b) Improvement by Atlas**: Δ distributions. CC200 mean Δ = +0.047, CC400 mean Δ = +0.052. Both atlases show comparable improvement despite differing dimensionality.
**(c) Paired Comparison (CC200 vs CC400)**: Subject-matched scatter plot with group coloring. High inter-atlas correlation (r > 0.9) demonstrates that BS-NET correction is consistent across parcellation granularities.
**(d) Summary: r_FC vs ρ̂T by Atlas**: Grouped bar chart with error bars comparing raw and corrected performance. Both atlases show significant improvement, with ROI count annotations.

**Cross-dataset consistency**: ABIDE (N=468) ρ̂T = 0.843 vs ADHD (N=40) ρ̂T = 0.866. Independent datasets confirm consistent BS-NET improvement.
