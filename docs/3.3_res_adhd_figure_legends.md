### Supplementary Figure S6. Reliability-Aware Clustering Utility (Exploratory)
**File**: `docs/figure/FigS6_Reliability_Aware_Clustering.png`  
**Script**: `src/visualization/plot_patient_utility_clustering.py`  
**Source data**: `data/adhd/pcp/results/adhd200_reliability_clustering_summary.csv`

- 목적: `ρ̂T` strata(T1/T2/T3)에서 비지도 HC/Patients 정렬도(ARI, BalAcc)를 탐색적으로 제시
- 구성:
  - A: KMeans ARI (method별 grouped bar + p annotation)
  - B: KMeans Balanced Accuracy (method별 grouped bar + p annotation)
  - C: ARI heatmap (strata × FC method)
- 해석 가드레일: 워터마크/캡션으로 non-robust significance 경고를 명시

### Supplementary Figure S7. Reliability-Aware Supervised Discrimination (LOSO)
**File**: `docs/figure/FigS7_Reliability_Aware_Classification.png`  
**Script**: `src/visualization/plot_patient_utility_classification.py`  
**Source data**:
- `data/adhd/pcp/results/adhd200_reliability_classification_summary_loso_nobal_nocov_perm1000_repeat10.csv`
- `data/adhd/pcp/results/adhd200_reliability_classification_runs_loso_nobal_nocov_perm1000_repeat10.csv`

- 목적: 인용 가이드 기반 설정(LOSO, tangent, logistic_l2, permutation)에서 supervised discrimination 신호를 보조 증거로 제시
- 구성:
  - A: Primary BalAcc (strata별, permutation p annotation)
  - B: Primary AUC (strata별, permutation p annotation)
  - C: BalAcc heatmap (FC method 비교, model 고정)
  - D: Primary p-value 패널(BalAcc/AUC, p<0.05 기준선)
- 해석 가드레일:
  - clinical biomarker claim이 아닌 exploratory utility evidence
  - BalAcc를 primary endpoint로, AUC는 supportive metric으로 해석
